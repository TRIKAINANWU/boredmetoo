Frequently Asked Questions

How long will my order take?

Orders are printed and shipped within 2-3 business days. Total delivery time is typically 7-10 days depending on your location.

What colors are available?

We offer the following colors: Red-Black, Burnt Titanium, Twinkling Silver, Red, Black, Blue, Yellow, Green, Purple, Rainbow, Chief's Red-Yellow, Blue-Green, Copper-Purple-Green, White, Grey, and Orange. Just leave a note with your color preference at checkout.

*Please note: Rainbow filament produces unique color patterns with each print, so your item may not look identical to others.

Can I return or exchange items?

Yes! We offer a 30-day return/exchange policy. If you're not satisfied with your purchase, contact us and we'll make it right.

How do I care for my 3D printed items?

PLA is durable but should be kept away from extreme heat (don't leave in hot cars). Clean with mild soap and water. Avoid dishwashers and harsh chemicals.

Do you take custom orders?

Absolutely! Contact us at boredmet2@gmail.com to discuss custom designs, bulk orders, or special requests.
